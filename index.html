What's inside

01

The Beginner Primer

What a solar return chart actually is, what you're looking at, and why it matters. Plain-English foundation — signs, planets, houses — before you ever open astro.com.

02

Cast Your Chart

Step-by-step walkthrough on astro.com. Your birth data, your birthday location, and you're in. We cover which house system to use and why it changes everything.

03

Your Rising Sign & Ruling Planet

The SR rising sign sets the tone for the entire year — the role you're stepping into, the energy you're embodying. Find it, find its ruling planet, and know what that combination is telling you.

04

Sun & Moon

Where your energy is most alive and where your emotional work lives this year. Two placements, two different kinds of information — together they shape how the whole year feels.

05

Reading the Houses

Every activated house is an area of life asking for your attention. Learn to read what's lit up, what the empty houses mean, and how to spot the push-pull between areas competing for your focus.

06

The Planets

Ten planets, ten kinds of energy. Before you can read the relationships between them, you need to know who they are — what each one wants, what it brings, and what it means when it shows up prominently in your year.

07

Aspects: Lines on the Chart

The lines between planets show you where things flow and where things push. You don't need to fear the friction lines — you need to know what they're asking for.

08

Planetary Magic

The chart isn't just for reading — it's for working with. Planetary day and hour rulers for timing your intentions, body correspondences, plant allies for each planet, and the thread that connects astrology to alchemy. Welcome to witchcraft, baby.
