Module Notes

One question to sit with per module. Take notes as you go — or come back after you've had time to sit with the material.

01 The Beginner Primer

Which of the four pieces (signs, planets, houses, aspects) feels most unfamiliar — and most interesting?

02 Cast Your Chart

What's your first reaction when you look at your chart? What draws your eye?

03 Rising Sign & Ruling Planet

What does your SR Rising sign say about the energy of this year? Does the ruling planet's house placement feel accurate?

04 Sun & Moon

Where is your Sun going this year — and does that feel like relief or resistance? What about your Moon's emotional home?

05 Reading the Houses

Which house is most crowded this year? Which is empty — and what sign sits on that cusp?

06 The Planets

Which planet feels most prominent in your chart this year? What does that planet want?

07 Aspects: Lines on the Chart

What are the dominant lines you see — blue (ease) or red (friction)? What are they connecting?

08 Planetary Magic

Which planet's magic feels most accessible to work with this year — and how might you bring it in?
