Your Year Ahead  ·  A Self-Paced Course

Your birthday gives you
a new map every year.
Learn to read it.

A solar return chart resets on your birthday — a snapshot of the sky that maps the year ahead. This course teaches you to cast it yourself, read the placements that matter most, and walk away with a framework you'll use for the rest of your life.

Self-paced · Written · Return every birthday

You've probably had your chart read by someone else — or Googled a placement and gotten a paragraph that felt close but not quite right. That's because no one knows your life like you do.

This course is going to show you the ingredients you have. You're the one who makes the potion.

The solar return chart isn't a prediction. It's a map of available energy — which areas of your life are lit up, where the ease is, where the friction is, what themes are likely to shape the year. What you do with that is yours.

This course gives you the framework to read that map for yourself, every year, for the rest of your life.

What's inside

Eight modules + a capstone tool.

Work through them in order — each one builds on the last. You can always browse and jump around from the module menu.

01

The Beginner Primer

Signs, planets, houses, aspects — the four pieces of the chart. Plain-language foundation, no prior astrology knowledge assumed.

02

Cast Your Chart

Step-by-step walkthrough on astro.com. No math, no guesswork — just your birth data, your birthday location, and fifteen minutes.

03

Your Rising Sign & Ruling Planet

The solar return rising sets the tone for the entire year. Find yours, find its ruling planet, understand what they're saying together.

04

Sun & Moon

Where your energy wants to go and where your emotional world will live this year. Two placements that tell you how the year will feel from the inside.

05

Reading the Houses

Which areas of life are activated — and which are asking for rest. Crowded houses and empty ones both mean something. We cover both.

06

The Planets

Ten planets, ten kinds of energy. Know who they are before you read the relationships between them.

07

Aspects: Lines on the Chart

Blue lines are ease. Red lines are friction. Neither is bad — both are useful. This module shows you how to read what's happening between the planets.

08

Planetary Magic

The chart isn't just for reading — it's for working with. Day and hour rulers for timing your intentions, body correspondences, plant allies for each planet. Welcome to witchcraft, baby.

✦

Your Year Ahead Snapshot

The capstone. An interactive tool that generates a personalized reading from your three key placements. Print it, save it, return to it all year long.

About Staley

Eighteen years of astrology. Ten years of witchcraft. Several very illuminating conversations with animals — and a few with people who were no longer technically here.

I'm a UX designer by day and a full-time practitioner of the mystical by everything else. I trained at the Boulder Psychic Institute — learning to read, release, and renew my own energy and the energy of others. Whimsy is my antidote to attempted conformity. My goal is to give you tools that take the mystical and bring it into the mundane, because the deep belief that holds all of this together is simple: knowledge is the least scary thing in the world.

I'm glad you're here. ♡
