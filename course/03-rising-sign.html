What your SR Rising sign tells you

In Module 02 you found the letters "AC" or "ASC" on the left side of your chart wheel. The sign sitting at that point is your SR Rising — your solar return Ascendant.

In astrology, the Ascendant is always about how something presents itself. In your natal chart, it's how you present yourself — your instinctive style, your first impression, the mask you wear before people get to know you.

In your solar return chart, the Ascendant does the same thing — but for the year instead of for your life. It sets the tone. It describes the role you're stepping into, the energy or attitude you'll be embodying, the general theme or mood of the next twelve months. It's what others will start to notice about you. It's how you'll tend to initiate new experiences.

The SR Rising is the costume the year is wearing. It shapes everything you encounter while you're in it.

It's not the most important placement in the solar return chart — that's the SR Sun, which we cover in Module 04. But it's the frame. And knowing the frame changes how you read everything inside it.

A note if you're working without a birth time

The SR Rising sign is the placement most affected by an uncertain birth time. Because the Ascendant moves through all twelve signs in a single day, even an hour's difference can shift it into a different sign entirely.

If you're working from an estimated or unknown birth time, read the sign that appeared on your chart — but hold it lightly. Notice if it resonates. If it doesn't feel right at all, the adjacent signs are worth looking at too.

Everything else in this module — the ruling planet, its house placement — still applies and is still worth exploring. An uncertain Ascendant doesn't cancel the rest of the work.

The 12 SR Rising signs

Find your SR Rising sign below. Read it as the year's theme — not as a personality type, and not as a prediction. Read it as: this is the territory I'm moving through.

The herbs listed under each sign are that sign's plant allies for the year. Work with them however resonates — in tea, as incense, on an altar, or just as a reference point. Use what calls to you. Leave the rest.

Aries Rising

You're stepping into a year of bold initiation. Aries rising sets an energized, action-forward tone — you're showing up first, moving fast, willing to start things without a roadmap. There's a sharpness to you this year, a readiness to lead or go first when no one else will.

The theme is beginnings. New territory. Courage as something you practice, not just feel. This is a year to move toward what you want without waiting for the conditions to be perfect. They won't be. Move anyway.

Herbs ✦ betony (wood), blackberry, blessed thistle, chili powder, cowslips, juniper, loosestrife, marjoram, mushrooms

Taurus Rising

The year slows down into something deliberate. Taurus rising brings a grounded, sensory, accumulating quality to everything — you're building, settling in, paying attention to what you actually have. Others notice a steadiness to you, a new attentiveness to comfort and beauty and what's worth keeping.

The theme is patience as a practice, pleasure as a valid tool. What are you cultivating this year? What do you actually value? This is a year to move at your own pace and mean it — and to notice that slow and solid is its own kind of power.

Herbs ✦ apple blossom, cumin, fig-wort, lovage

Gemini Rising

Quick, curious, communicative. Gemini rising sets a year of information-gathering, connection-making, and moving fluidly between worlds. You're showing up as someone who asks good questions, notices patterns, adapts fast. Others notice your words, your wit, your ability to be genuinely interested in almost anything.

The theme is learning and expressing — thinking out loud as part of the work, being okay with not having the final answer yet. This is a year where the conversation itself is the destination.

Herbs ✦ dragon's blood reeds, meadowsweet

Cancer Rising

Cancer rising brings a protective, emotionally attuned quality to how you're showing up — you're leading with feeling this year, with care, with an instinct to safeguard what matters. Home, belonging, roots, and the people who feel like family are all front and center.

Others may notice a softer edge to you, or a stronger impulse to protect your inner world. The theme is nourishment — of yourself, of the relationships and places you love, of whatever you're quietly tending to.

Leo Rising

You're being called forward — into visibility, into warmth, into full self-expression. Leo rising sets a year where showing up fully is the assignment. Others notice your presence, your generosity, your creative spark. There's a brightness to you this year that's hard to miss.

The theme is radiance — not performance, not seeking approval, but authentic shine. This is a year to take up space without apology, to lead from the heart, to let yourself be seen doing the thing you love. The world benefits when you stop dimming.

Virgo Rising

The year asks for discernment, craft, and care. Virgo rising brings a focused, analytical, service-oriented quality to how you move through the world. You're attending to the details — what's working, what needs refinement, what you're called to improve. Others notice your precision, your reliability, your high standards.

The theme is skill — doing the thing well, not just doing it. This is a year where the quality of your attention becomes the quality of your output. Small adjustments compound. The work repays the care you put into it.

Herbs ✦ lavender, maidenhair

Libra Rising

Partnership, beauty, and balance move to the foreground. Libra rising sets a year where relationship — with others, with aesthetics, with fairness — is the operating system. You're showing up as a collaborator, a connector, someone who cares how things look and feel and whether they're working for everyone involved.

Others notice your grace, your consideration, your eye for what's beautiful. The theme is relating — what you're creating alongside other people, and what it costs you to keep the peace versus what it costs you to stop.

Herbs ✦ geranium (rose), horse-tongue, verbena, vervain

Scorpio Rising

Depth, intensity, and transformation. Scorpio rising sets a year where the surface isn't the point — you're going underneath things, asking harder questions, refusing easy answers and comfortable half-truths. Others notice a magnetic quality to you this year, a seriousness, a sense of power held close.

The theme is excavation — what's hidden, what's real, what can't be avoided any longer. This is a year of meaningful change. Not dramatic for its own sake, but honest in a way that rearranges things. You'll emerge knowing something you can't unknow.

Others notice your enthusiasm, your vision, your need for room to roam.

The theme is belief — what you're reaching toward and why, what framework you're building for how the world works, what it means to live according to something larger than your own comfort. This is a year to go further than you planned.

You're showing up as someone who's working toward something — disciplined, strategic, committed to the result. Others notice your focus, your reliability, a quiet authority that wasn't quite there before.

The theme is legacy — what you're constructing that will last, what you're willing to do the unglamorous work for, what success actually looks like when you define it yourself. This is a year where patience and structure pay off.

The year breaks from convention. Aquarius rising brings an independent, forward-thinking, collective-minded quality to how you move through the world. You're showing up as someone with their own angle — original, community-oriented, maybe a little detached from the personal in favor of the broader picture. Others notice your distinctness, your ideas, your refusal to follow scripts.

The theme is innovation — what's possible when you stop doing it the expected way, what you can contribute when you're willing to be a little strange, what it means to belong to something larger than yourself without losing your own frequency.

You're showing up with less armor and more permeability — sensing things before you can name them, moving by feel, dissolving the line between what's yours and what belongs to the room. Others notice a softness, a creativity, something a little otherworldly.

The theme is surrender — trusting what you can't see, following the current instead of fighting it, letting go of the need to control the outcome in order to be fully inside the experience. This is a year for the invisible work. It counts even when it can't be measured.

Herbs ✦ alder (common), hemp, moss, sphagnum

Your ruling planet

Every sign has a ruling planet — a planet that carries that sign's energy most naturally, that feels most at home in it. When a sign becomes your SR Rising, its ruling planet becomes what astrologers call the chart ruler for the year: a second key for reading the year's theme.

Think of it this way: the SR Rising sets the overall tone, and the chart ruler adds texture and direction to that tone. Where the ruling planet lands in your chart — which house, which sign — tells you something about how the year's theme will actually play out in your life.

Finding your ruling planet in the chart

Now go back to your solar return chart and find the glyph for your ruling planet. Each planet has a symbol — here are the most common ones to look for:

Personal planets

The inner ones

☉ Sun · ☽ Moon · ☿ Mercury · ♀ Venus · ♂ Mars. These move faster, so they're more personal in their effects.

Social & outer planets

The slower ones

♃ Jupiter · ♄ Saturn · ♅ Uranus · ♆ Neptune · ♇ Pluto. These move slowly and shape longer cycles. Uranus, Neptune, and Pluto are often small and close together on the chart — look carefully.

Once you've found your ruling planet, note two things: which house it's in, and which sign it's in.

The house your ruling planet occupies is where the year's central theme is playing out. The sign it's in tells you how.

For example: Gemini rising with Mercury in the 10th house in Capricorn. The year's theme is communication and learning — and it's happening in the arena of career and public reputation, with a serious, disciplined, results-oriented flavor. That's a year where how you speak and what you say at work could shift your trajectory.

You don't need to know every nuance of every planet in every sign right now. Just locate your ruling planet, note where it is, and sit with what that might mean given what you already know. We'll build on this as we go.
