Aspects

Trine

120° · Blue lines

Ease and flow. The energies support each other naturally.

Sextile

60° · Blue lines

Ease with some effort. Opportunity that rewards a nudge.

Conjunction

0° · Same point

Blending. The two planets merge into one energy, operating as one.

Square

90° · Red lines

Friction for growth. Tension that forces mastery or a workaround.

Opposition

180° · Red lines

Push-pull between two areas of life. Integration is the work.

A note on friction

Squares & oppositions

Neither is bad. Friction aspects show where the real growth is happening.
